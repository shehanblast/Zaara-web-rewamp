Malaysia Tour

Description

A five-day tour of the most iconic Asian city will be a clever blend of history, culture, heritage and entertainment. Kuala Lumpur is Malaysia’s capital city and is known to be one of the main hubs
to access a majority of the other Asian nations. Colloquially referred to as ‘KL’, keen travellers will be able to immerse themselves in a diverse local culture
that combines charms of the Chinese, Malays and Indians; sample a variety of cuisine and visit different religious shrines.
The city’s iconic Petronas Twin Towers stands at 451m in height and the viewing deck offers the best bird’s eye view of the sprawling, historic city.


Activities Coverd

• Experience Full Day Sunway Lagoon
• Experience a Half Day City Tour
• Half Day Putrajaya Tour



Locations Covered

• Kuala Lumpur
