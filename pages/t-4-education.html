Educational Tourism

Learn Through Travel

Zaara Tours Educational tourism can be defined as tourism that pursues educational learning as a pretext for a trip, either learning knowledge — historical, cultural, social — or learning a language more and more practiced every day.

Education has used tourism as a tool to complement learning, accumulating experiences from practice. Sri Lanka's rich history, diverse ecosystems, and vibrant culture make it an ideal destination for educational journeys that go far beyond the classroom.

From ancient kingdoms and UNESCO World Heritage Sites to wildlife sanctuaries and tea country — every destination becomes a living lesson. Zaara Tours crafts programmes tailored to schools, universities, and learning groups of all kinds.
