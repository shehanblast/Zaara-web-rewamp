Our Story

Zaara Tours International is managed and staffed by a young and dynamic group of experienced professionals in the trade, who are committed towards providing professional tours with memorable holidays.

Showcasing many extraordinary hotels and villas in Sri Lanka & outside Sri Lanka (internationally), Zaara Tours International provides Sports Tours, Luxury Vehicles, Helicopter Travel, Water Sports, Wild Life Tours, Heritage Tours and unparalleled access to exclusive offers and insider tips for the sophisticated traveler combined with Outbound Tours.

Our memberships include the Sri Lanka Tourist Development Authority, Chauffeur Tourist Guide Association of Inbound Tour Operators and Wedding Suppliers Association of Sri Lanka.
