Who We Are

Zaara Event Media was incorporated in the year 2010. The objective being to provide an excellent service for Event Management, which revolves in organising Events and Conventions to the utmost satisfaction and convenience of customers.

Our Events

Apart from the core features mentioned above, our company has been successful in organising events such as Product Launches, Gala Dinners, Special Celebrity Entertainments, Musical Shows, Conferences, Conventions, Weddings, Concerts, Promotional Campaigns, Media Launches, Dinner Dances and a host of other events to suit both individual and corporate clients.
