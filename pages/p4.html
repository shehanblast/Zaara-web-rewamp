10 Nights 11 Days Package

The most comprehensive Sri Lanka experience — from misty highlands and ancient ruins to wildlife safaris, golden beaches and the vibrant streets of Colombo. Eleven days that leave nothing behind.

Day by Day

Your Journey Awaits

1

Day 1

Arrival → Negombo

Route

Airport → Negombo

Drive

30 min – 1 hr

Hotel

Negombo beach hotel

  • •Ayubowan (May you live long!) — your friendly driver/guide will welcome you at the airport.
  • •Transfer to the charming fishing village of Negombo, conveniently located near the airport.
  • •Relax after your journey — Negombo's coastal city has a rich history and beautiful beaches to explore.
2

Day 2

Negombo → Habarana → Dambulla

Route

Negombo → Habarana → Dambulla

Drive

~3 hrs

Hotel

Hotel in Sigiriya/Habarana

  • •After breakfast, visit one of the country's busiest fishing centres with colourful rural boats sailing in the sea.
  • •City tour of Negombo: Dutch period architecture, canals, a Buddhist temple and a Catholic church.
  • •Depart to the cultural triangle — visit the Dambulla Cave Temple, a stunning UNESCO World Heritage site.
3

Day 3

Polonnaruwa → Sigiriya

Route

Polonnaruwa → Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Drive

~1.5 hrs

Hotel

Hotel in Habarana/Sigiriya

  • •Visit Sigiriya Rock Fortress UNESCO World Heritage site — water gardens, ancient frescoes and graffiti.
  • •Afternoon visit Polonnaruwa ancient city — monasteries, Buddhist remains and the magnificent Gal Vihara rock temple.
  • •Optional: Jeep safari at Minneriya National Park or a peaceful rural village experience.
4

Day 4

Dambulla → Matale → Kandy

Route

Dambulla → Matale → Kandy

Drive

~3 hrs

Hotel

Hotel in Kandy

  • •Visit Matale Aluvihare Temple where Buddhist doctrines were first written on Ola leaves in the 1st century.
  • •Explore a Spice Garden at Matale before continuing to Kandy.
  • •Visit the Temple of the Tooth Relic UNESCO World Heritage site, and see famous Blue & Star sapphires being cut and polished.
  • •End the day enjoying a traditional culture show in Kandy.
5

Day 5

Kandy City Tour

Route

Kandy city tour

Drive

Local

Hotel

Hotel in Kandy

  • •Visit Peradeniya Botanical Garden — over 4,000 species of plants including orchids, spices, medicinal plants and towering palm trees.
  • •Tour around Kandy: beautiful lake, colourful streets, colonial architecture, museums, arts & crafts workshop, oriental market and a bazaar.
6

Day 6

Kandy → Nuwara Eliya

Route

Kandy → Nuwara Eliya

Drive

~2 hrs

Hotel

Hotel in Nuwara Eliya

  • •Head to Nuwara Eliya — the 'little England' with Victorian-era structures, tea plantations, waterfalls and mountain ranges.
  • •Visit a Tea Factory to see how world-renowned Ceylon tea is produced from leaf to cup.
  • •Evening stroll through the Local Market and a walk along scenic Gregory Lake.
7

Day 7

Horton Plains / World's End

Route

Horton Plains National Park

Drive

~1 hr

Hotel

Hotel in Nuwara Eliya

  • •Depart early to Horton Plains — a stunning highland plateau at 2,100–2,300m altitude, rich in endemic biodiversity.
  • •Main attraction: World's End — a breathtaking cliff that drops 880 metres to the valley below.
  • •On a clear morning, views extend over miniature tea plantation villages all the way to the south coast.
8

Day 8

Ella → Kataragama → Yala

Route

Ella → Kataragama → Yala

Drive

~3 hrs

Hotel

Hotel in Tissa/Yala

  • •Scenic drive through Ella — visit Ella Gap & Rawana Falls along the way.
  • •Visit Kataragama Hindu Kovil — an ancient pilgrimage site revered by Buddhists, Hindus, Muslims and Christians alike.
  • •Arrive at Yala and prepare for an evening jeep safari through the tropical forest bordering the sea.
9

Day 9

Yala National Park Safari

Route

Yala National Park

Drive

Local

Hotel

Hotel in Tissa/Yala

  • •Early morning jeep safari at Yala National Park — Sri Lanka's most visited wildlife reserve.
  • •Spot the elusive Sri Lankan Leopard, Asian Elephant, and many other mammals and birds in their natural habitat.
10

Day 10

Yala → Hambantota → Galle

Route

Yala → Hambantota → Galle

Drive

~2 hrs

Hotel

Hotel in South Coast Beach

  • •Visit the famous stilt fishermen, Salt Refinery and Fishing Harbour in Hambantota on the way to Galle.
  • •Galle city tour: UNESCO World Heritage Site — the iconic Dutch Fort, Galle Lighthouse and tiny historical back streets.
  • •Browse quirky boutiques and stop at a local café for lunch. Visit Galle National Museum.
11

Day 11

Colombo – Departure

Your final day in Sri Lanka — a city send-off before your flight home.

  • •After breakfast, depart to Colombo via the coastal road.
  • •En-route visit Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery — Sri Lanka's pioneering sea turtle conservation project.
  • •Colombo city tour: Fort, Galle Face Green, Pettah Dutch architecture, Independence Square, Old Parliament House and Beira Lake.
  • •Last-minute shopping at House of Fashion or Odel before your onward journey.
