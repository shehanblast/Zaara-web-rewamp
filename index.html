Who We Are

Welcome to Zaara

Zaara Tours International is managed and staffed by a young and dynamic group of experienced professionals in the trade, committed to providing professional tours with memorable holidays.

Showcasing extraordinary hotels and villas in Sri Lanka & internationally, we offer Sports Tours, Luxury Vehicles, Helicopter Travel, Water Sports, Wild Life Tours, Heritage Tours and exclusive insider access. Our memberships include the Sri Lanka Tourist Development Authority, Chauffeur Tourist Guide Association, and the Wedding Suppliers Association of Sri Lanka.
